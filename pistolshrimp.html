PISTOL SHRIMP


Skills: Conceptual Development, Web DesignGraphic Design, Interactive Design, Sound Design, Musical Composition, Motion Graphics

Tools:Ableton Live, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Processing, Arduino
Pistol Shrimp is a net label project me and a group of colleagues developed while having in mind the impact of the internet on musical distribution.
It’s main focus is the role an interactive experience can portray in order to engage the listener / user during the experience of music and visual stimuli.
All the releases came with an interactive momentum, similar to a game level where the purpose is to find out the download.
The Interactive Dancefloor was also a piece developed inside this project, where the creation of a life-size out of the screen experience embodies a space where people can stand on and generate sound.
This was a team project done by me (Mariana Freitas), João Rodrigues, Margarida Botelho and Sandrina Pereira.
