WATER - 4.0 SURROUND SOUND


Skills: Sound Design, Musical Composition, Mixing, Mastering, Audio Spatialization

Tools: Ableton Live, CSound, Max/MSP
Water is a 17 minutes acousmatic piece composed for a quadraphonic sound system. As a spatial piece, it explores the circular movement of a sound mass without gaps by layering different frequencies in different places of the room.
Conceptually, the main focus of this piece was to generate an environment resemblant to that of being under water and resurfacing. Sound sources vary from field recordings to digital audio generated with CSound.
Here you can hear a 4 part stereo version of the original piece.
