AGORA IRREPETÍVEL - DOCUMENTARY


Skills: Sound Design, Musical Composition, Mixing, Mastering
Tools:Ableton Live
A Juventude Por Ela Própria (The Youth By Itself) is a 30min documentary about the work develop in the last semester by the finalists of the Communication Design BA at the Fine Arts Faculty in Lisbon.
It was screened during the opening of the exibhition Agora Irrepetível (Now, Unrepeatable).
Here the biggest challenge was to compose a soundtrack that would provide identity to the documentary while not overpowering the constante monologues that are present through out the documentary.
This was a team project done by me (Mariana Freitas) and João Rodrigues.
