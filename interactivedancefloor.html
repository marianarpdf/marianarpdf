INTERACTIVE DANCEFLOOR


Skills: Interaction Design, Electronic Engineering, Programming, Sound Design
Tools: iMac, Ableton Live, Arduino, Wood, Aluminium, Coper Wire, Stereo Sound System
The Interactive Dancefloor is a life-sized midi controller working with Arduino and Ableton Live. Here we somewhat obliterate the role of the musician, giving the people the chance to be the performer and the composer of the musical piece.
All electronics and sound-design was made by our team in order to allow an interactive experience where is dancing that brings music to life and not the other way around.
12 different “pads” are able to control up to 12 different instruments equalized to work together, generating a complex and rich sound, ranging from ambient textures to deep basses and hard kicking drums.
This was a team project done by me (Mariana Freitas), João Rodrigues, Margarida Botelho and Sandrina Pereira.
