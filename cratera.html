CRATERA


Skills: Sound Design, Musical Composition, Graphic Design, Branding, Design Management
Tools: Ableton Live, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Illustrator, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript
Born in 2016, CRATERA is a digital record label based in Lisbon, Portugal.
It’s main focus is the online distribution of electronic music and the creation of live interactive experiences.
Being the brainchild of three peculiar individuals - all of them designers with experience in digital development and interactive projects - CRATERA’s graphic image was a true challenge, as it had to embrace both a clean and minimal look that is compatible with the web, while maintaining some sort of playfulness and interaction with the viewer.
This was a team project done by me (Mariana Freitas), João Rodrigues and Margarida Botelho.
